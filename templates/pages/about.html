About Me

Hello, My name is Smokin Joe

Being raised in Florida with deep roots, I've spent my life immersed in the Sunshine State's vibrant culture and stunning landscapes. From humble beginnings in Southwest Florida, I've embraced a spirit of entrepreneurship, exploring various ventures from agriculture to retail.

With a heart for my community, I've dedicated myself to advocating for children and preserving Florida's unique heritage. As the host of the Cracker Culture Podcast on Smokin Joe Nation YouTube, I share stories, insights, and a passion for all things Florida. Join me as we celebrate the spirit of the Sunshine State!

Subscribe to the Cracker Culture Podcast and let's connect on the journey of discovering Florida's true essence.
